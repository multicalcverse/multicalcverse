Simple Interest Calculator

Quick Explanation

This calculator helps you quickly calculate simple interest based on principal amount, rate of interest, and time period.

Introduction

The Simple Interest Calculator is a useful financial tool that allows users to calculate interest easily without complex formulas.

How This Calculator Works

Enter the principal amount, rate of interest, and time period. The calculator instantly shows the interest and total amount.

Formula Used

Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

Example

If you invest ₹1,000 at 5% interest for 2 years, the simple interest will be ₹100 and the total amount will be ₹1,100.

Use Cases

  • Students learning basic finance
  • Bank interest estimation
  • Quick personal finance calculations

FAQs

What is simple interest?
Simple interest is calculated only on the principal amount.

Who can use this calculator?
Anyone who wants a quick interest calculation.
