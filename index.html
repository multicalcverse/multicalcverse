Simple Interest Calculator for Students in India

This simple interest calculator helps students in India calculate interest easily based on principal amount, rate of interest, and time period.

Calculator Inputs

Explanation

This calculator uses the simple interest formula to calculate interest on the principal amount for a fixed period of time.

Simple Interest Formula

Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

Example

If a student invests ₹10,000 at a rate of 5% per year for 2 years, the simple interest will be ₹1,000 and the total amount will be ₹11,000.

FAQs

What is simple interest?

Simple interest is calculated only on the principal amount and does not include interest on previously earned interest.

Who should use this calculator?

Students learning basic interest calculations can use this calculator.

Is this calculator accurate for India?

Yes, it follows the standard simple interest formula used in India.
